Tutti Frutti Bread Recipe

The fruit bread is baked to perfection and a slice is perfect with a cup of milk. PREP TIME15 mins COOK TIME45 mins RESTING TIME2 hrs. TOTAL TIME3 hrs. COURSE Main Course CUISINE American INGREDIENTS • ▢Warm Milk - 1 ¼ cup • ▢Sugar - 3 tbsp. • ▢Dry Yeast - 2 tsp • ▢All Purpose Flour / Maida - 2 cups + more as needed • ▢Wheat Flour - 1 cup • ▢Salt - ¼ tsp • ▢Butter - 4 tbsp. • ▢Tutti Frutti - ¾ cup INSTRUCTIONS • ▢ Heat milk in a saucepan till warm, add butter and allow it to melt.
Set aside to cool down.
• ▢ Add sugar and mix well. Add in yeast and set aside for 5 mins so the yeast activates.
• ▢ Now take flour, wheat flour, salt in a bowl and whisk well.
• ▢ Add in the tutti Frutti and mix well.
• ▢ Add the milk mix and knead to a dough.
• ▢ Cover and set aside for 1 hour.
• ▢ Now punch back the air out and knead well.
• ▢ Now spread it like a square in a floured surface and roll it tightly. Place it in a greased loaf pan.
• ▢ Let it rise for 30 mins. During the last 10 mins of rising, preheat oven to 190 degree C / 375-degree F. oven.
• ▢ Now brush the top with milk and bake for 35 to 40 mins in a 190 degree C / 375-degree F. oven.
If is getting too dark, place a sheet of foil on top.
• ▢ Remove from oven and cool completely. Slice and serve.
